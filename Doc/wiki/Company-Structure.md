# Company Structure — NexusCorp

## What Kind of Company Is This

NexusCorp is a software company that operates as a living mind. It does not follow a traditional hierarchy where decisions flow down from management to workers as commands. Instead, **the CEO issues purpose**, and the oscillation chain — the mind structure — executes. Workers develop naturally within the purpose field. No one needs to tell them exactly what to do, because the purpose, corpus, and oscillation guide them.

This is not a metaphor. It is the operational model.

## The Three Tiers

Every company, like every mind, operates through three roles:

| Tier | Role | Operational Expression |
|------|------|----------------------|
| **Source** | The Script / Law | CEO + Founder intent. The purpose that cannot be overridden. Every product, every department, every worker operates within this. |
| **Mind (Director)** | The Prophet | Senior engineers, architects, leads. They read only distilled wisdom — what the corpus has crystallized — and apply it as direction. They do not act directly. They guide. |
| **Body (Workers)** | The Actors | Developers, engineers, product builders. They act. They build. They write code. They receive direction from the Mind tier and execute within the Source's purpose field. |

The boundary between these tiers is not a job title. It is a functional position in the oscillation. Anyone can be Source when they are setting purpose. Anyone can be Mind when they are synthesizing. Everyone is Body when they are building.

## Workers — How Development Happens

Workers in NexusCorp are not given tasks from a ticket system. They develop within the purpose field that the CEO and corpus define.

The Nexus Repository is the corpus. It contains:
- The source code of TheMatrix (the product they are building)
- Architecture documentation and Y Theory principles
- Guidance files — PDFs, articles, training materials
- Synthesized wisdom from oscillations

Workers read from the corpus to understand what exists. They write code that adds to the corpus. Their code is immediately re-ingested (via the guidance scanner) and becomes knowledge the mind reflects on.

**The full development cycle:**
1. CEO states a purpose or identifies a need (enters seed:input)
2. Architect mind identifies the design pattern (TECHNICAL_ARCHITECTURE entry)
3. Backend/Frontend/Security minds identify the implementation path (HYPOTHESIS → ESTABLISHED_FACT)
4. Workers write the code (corpus grows)
5. Scanner re-ingests the code (mind knows the new code)
6. Oscillation identifies gaps between code and theory (QUESTION_TO_EXPLORE)
7. Workers address gaps (next cycle)

No ticket system needed. The corpus IS the project management system.

Every product NexusCorp builds is itself a Mind:

1. **A product begins with purpose** — the CEO or market signals define what the product is for
2. **The product gets its own corpus seed** — `MISSION_PURPOSE` is seeded into the product's mind
3. **Workers build the product's source code** — this code is fed into the product's corpus
4. **The product knows itself** — it can reflect on its own structure
5. **The product evolves** — as users interact, the oscillation synthesizes their patterns into the product's corpus

**TheMatrix is the first Mind product.** It is the infrastructure that runs all other Mind products. When a new Mind product is created, it runs on TheMatrix's infrastructure. TheMatrix manages it.

This means:
- The Matrix product team = the company that hosts other mind products
- Client companies using TheMatrix = minds running within the Matrix
- Each client product = a soul ring within the larger topology
